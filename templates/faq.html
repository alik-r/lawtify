Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and platform.

What services do you provide?

We offer appointment booking with legal experts, an AI-powered chatbot for simulated legal advice, and a library of resources including FAQs and guides on common legal issues.

Is this service free?

Initial consultations and chatbot sessions are completely free. If you choose to book a full service with a partner professional, standard fees may apply.

Is my information private?

Absolutely. We prioritize data privacy and use industry-standard practices to protect your personal information. Submissions are stored securely and not shared with third parties without consent.

How accurate is the AI advice?

The AI provides simulated responses based on general legal frameworks of Azerbaijan and post-Soviet regions for educational purposes. It's not a substitute for professional legal counsel.

Have more questions?

Feel free to reach out or start a chat with our AI assistant for personalized responses.
